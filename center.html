Account Setup Guide

Get your account configured correctly from day one.

Creating Your Account

  1. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
  2. Select "Create Account" on the sign-up form
  3. Fill in your details:

    • Username - Unique identifier (only letters, numbers, underscores)

Sparks System ✨

Your rewards for learning and engagement.

What are Sparks?

Sparks are points you earn for completing learning activities. They represent your progress and engagement on the platform.

How to Earn Sparks

  • Complete Lessons - 5-20 Sparks per lesson
  • Correct Answers - 10 Sparks per correct answer (full points)
  • Retry Success - 5 Sparks per correct retry (half points)
  • Course Completion - 100 Sparks bonus
  • Daily Login Streak - Bonus Sparks for consecutive days

What Can You Do With Sparks?

  • Unlock special achievement badges
  • Access advanced courses
  • Get certificates of completion
  • Leaderboard recognition
  • Special rewards and discounts (coming soon)

Pro Tip

Consistency is key! Log in daily and complete at least one exercise to build your streak and maximize Sparks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click "Sign In" then "Forgot Password?" on the login form. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
Can I change my Pathfinder answers?
Yes! Go to your dashboard settings and select "Update Learning Preferences". You can retake the Pathfinder quiz at any time to update your recommendations.
How are Sparks calculated?
Sparks are awarded based on question difficulty and your attempt. First-time correct answers earn full points (10 Sparks). Retry correct answers earn half points (5 Sparks). Wrong answers earn 0 Sparks but show the correct answer for learning.
Can I download certificates?
Yes! Upon completing a course with at least 80% of exercises correct, you'll unlock a downloadable certificate. Find it in your dashboard under "Achievements".
What's the difference between Exercise and Exam mode?
Exercise mode gives you 10 random questions with immediate feedback. Exam mode gives you 30 questions with a timer and simulates a real assessment. Both contribute to Sparks but Exam mode has higher point values.
How do I create my own lessons?
The Lesson Studio is available for educators and advanced users. Contact support to request creator access, or if you already have access, click "Create Lesson" from the admin dashboard.
Can I use Akademia on mobile?
Absolutely! Akademia is fully responsive and works on all devices. For the best experience, use a modern browser like Chrome, Safari, or Firefox.
How do I track my progress?
Your dashboard shows detailed progress metrics including course completion, exercise scores, Sparks earned, and time spent learning. You can also view individual lesson progress from the course page.

Troubleshooting Guide

Common Issues and Solutions

❌ Can't log in to my account

✓ Double-check your email/username and password
✓ Use "Forgot Password" to reset
✓ Clear your browser cache and cookies
✓ Try a different browser or incognito mode

❌ Lessons won't load or save

✓ Check your internet connection
✓ Refresh the page
✓ Clear browser cache
✓ Disable ad-blockers temporarily
✓ Try using Chrome or Firefox

❌ Exercise answers not submitting

✓ Make sure you've selected/typed an answer
✓ Check that you have a stable internet connection
✓ Refresh and try again (your progress auto-saves)
✓ Contact support if the issue persists

❌ Sparks not updating

✓ Refresh your dashboard
✓ Complete a new exercise to trigger update
✓ Check your internet connection
✓ Wait a few minutes - sometimes there's a slight delay

❌ Can't upload profile picture

✓ Ensure image is under 2MB
✓ Use JPG, PNG, or GIF format
✓ Check image dimensions (square works best)
✓ Try a different image
