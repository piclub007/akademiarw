Account Setup Guide 📝

Get your account configured correctly from day one.

Creating Your Account

  1. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
  2. Select "Create Account" on the sign-up form
  3. Fill in your details (Full Name, Username, Email, Password)
  4. Upload a profile photo (optional)
  5. Click "Create Account" to finish

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

How do I reset my password?
Click "Sign In" then "Forgot Password?" on the login form. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link.
Can I change my Pathfinder answers?
Yes! Go to your dashboard settings and select "Update Learning Preferences" to retake the quiz.
How are Sparks calculated?
First-time correct: full points (10 Sparks). Retry correct: half points (5 Sparks). Wrong: 0 Sparks but shows correct answer.
Can I download certificates?
Yes! Upon completing a course with at least 80% correct, you'll unlock a downloadable certificate.
Is Akademia mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! Akademia is fully responsive and works on all devices with modern browsers.
